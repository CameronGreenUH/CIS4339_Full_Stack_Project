General Questions


Are your pets raised in an safe, legal, and ethical manner?

All our products are created in state-of-the-art laboratories with that comply with regulatory requirements. We have designed our product's developmental process to meet the minimum legal and regulatory conditions allowed.

What happens to defective pets?

All defective products are recycled by breaking down the remains back into the their most basic bio-mass equivilants.

Has Pandora provided products to customers for legally dubious purposes?

Pandora has presently, and will always maintain that our business dealings have consistently operated within international law. Any proof presented to the contrary is patently false and libelous.

Will we ever edit virus or bacteria genomes?

While Pandora LLC is an online pet designer and will not expand into bio-medicine, our parent company, Pandora Corp. does own a subsidiary whose primary focus is in applied biological egineering of microrganisms.
